About Us

Nimmi's Kitchen started with one simple idea: home-style food made the way it's meant to be — fresh, flavourful, and full of love. What began as cooking for family and friends has grown into a kitchen that now serves daily tiffins, festive specials, and catering for every occasion.

Every dish is prepared in small batches using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, with no shortcuts and no preservatives — just honest, homemade taste delivered straight to your table.
